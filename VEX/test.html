Team Rosters

Finalized VEX team rosters have been determined for the 2015 - 2016 school year. Due to large numbers of prospective members and very limited spots and funding, many students unfortunately did not make the cut. Membership was determined by seniority, work contribution, overall attitude, and meeting attendance.

This year, our members are distributed across two teams, Work Zero and Efficiency Zero, aptly named for our reputation as highly efficient, productive collaborations. /s Each team will be meeting separately to build their respective robots. Please consult with your team leader (first name listed) for meeting schedules and plans.
